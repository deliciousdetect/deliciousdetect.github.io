Cafe Rococo

This medium size coffee shop in Kirkland deserves a special mention. They have a lot of places to sit solo, or with a friend, and the wifi is really good too. I only had one coffee there during my visit, but I really liked it. I also had some food, which was surprisingly good for a coffee shop. The staff was really helpful and friendly anytime I needed something.

If you are looking for the best coffee shop in Kirkland, this is probably it.

http://www.rocococoffee.com/
