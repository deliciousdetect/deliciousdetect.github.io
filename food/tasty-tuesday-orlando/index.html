Tasty Tuesday

Always on the lookout for the best food experiences, my search brought me to Orlando, Florida of all places. On a gorgeous May evening I arrived to one of the best food truck rodeos I’d experienced in a long time! #TastyTuesday! Three cool bars are all next to each other and they have a large variety of craft beer and cocktails to enjoy with whatever food trucks catch your eye in the back parking lot. I highly recommend checking this out if you are in Orlando or visiting!

Search for “Tasty Tuesdays in the Milk District” on Facebook to find the upcoming schedule and location. Just remember all the action is hidden in the back, so don’t worry if you are in the right place. You will have a great time!

Sat May 23, 2015
