Tasting Notes March 9th, 2016

I stopped by the Beer Junction on a cold and rainy night to see what they were pouring…

Two Beers Fall Line Russian Imperial Stout

12% ABV
This is a great beer if you want something strong, smooth and dark. Very solid flavor. Would order again.

Stone Brewing Americano Stout

8.7% ABV
You’ll need a few sips to let this one settle on your palate. Once you are warmed up though, if you are seeking an espresso / coffee flavor, this is a solid choice.

Three Magnets Old Skook in the Woods Barley Wine

12.5%
This is a pretty tasty barley wine. Especially considering the ABV… There is not a lot going on flavor wise, but I think that BJ is pouring this one a little bit too cold, if I may be honest. I’m generally a fan of barley wine and have a preference for the releases that Fremont has put out in recent times. I’ve only recently heard about Three Magnets, and this is my first experience with any of their offerings.
